Problem:
Candidates can solve problems but fail interviews because they can’t explain their thinking.

App idea:
After solving a problem:

User records or types their explanation

App scores:

clarity

structure

assumptions

trade-offs

Why it helps:
Interviews judge communication as much as correctness.

Differentiator:
LeetCode doesn’t train thinking out loud.

“Common Follow-Ups” – Interviewer Playbook

Problem:
Candidates are surprised by follow-up questions.

App idea:
For each problem:

Show common interviewer follow-ups

Ask user to respond

Examples:

“What if input is huge?”

“Can you do it in-place?”

“What if data is streaming?”
